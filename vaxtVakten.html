Växtvakten

Växtvakten is created in Kotlin by Me, Luk Kabamba and Christian Westesson as a group project for our education. It's a great app for those who's having trouble with keeping up with their plants at home. you can use our already made list of plant or add your own if you dont find it in the list. You can simple take a picture, give it a name and choose how often you want to give it water. After watering the flowers you can simple relax and wait for the notification to tell you when it's time to give them water again.
In this app we used ROOM as storage for the flowers and Github for source control.
