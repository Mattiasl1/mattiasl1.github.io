Småföretagarnas Riksförbund

This prototype app was created during my internship at Levento Creative Consultans with my classmate Staffan Ljungqvist. we were asked to create an mvp app for Småföretagarnas Riksförbund which is a national federation for entrepreneurs. We started of by charting the features, designing the UI, presenting interactive wireframes, we wanted to make the app available for both iOS and Android and I was tasked with developing the iOS app which I decided to make in SwiftUI.
The app's three main functions is ability to use a digital membership, a channel for relevant news and a databank for the special deals and offers.
All the content is made to be dynamic with continuous updates. News article posts are parsed from an RSS feed stemming from the already established webpage.
The database of offers from numerous companies are hosted on firebase, and are available to browse on the app sorted by category as well as featured content.
